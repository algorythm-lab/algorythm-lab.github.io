About Us

Algorythm Lab is a cutting-edge project that harnesses the power of conversational AI to revolutionize the study of Data Structures and Algorithms (DSA). Our mission is to make learning DSA an interactive, engaging, and accessible experience for students and professionals alike. By combining advanced AI technology with comprehensive DSA curriculum, we're paving the way for a new era of computer science education.

The Socratic method is a form of teaching through asking thought-provoking questions that lead students to discover answers and deepen their understanding. Originating from the Greek philosopher Socrates, this method has been practiced since the 5th century BCE to stimulate critical thinking and dialogue. The Socratic method enhances DSA learning by fostering active problem-solving and deeper comprehension, as it challenges students to think critically, question assumptions, and derive solutions through guided inquiry, rather than passively absorbing information.
